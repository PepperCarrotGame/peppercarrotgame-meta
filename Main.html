Hello there, we have been working on a game based on David Revoy's open source comic Pepper & Carrot

Progress:
For the last few months, we have a stablished story and game mechanics and we are currently looking for an artist to join us with the development of the title, we have a team of three versatile programmers and a composer for the music.

Mechanics:
The game is a 2D side-scrolling RPG game that narrates the epic quest of Pepper and company to restore order in their world after a misterious disappearance of their beloved pets.

Release:
The title is intended release on Windows and Linux on the Steam and GOG platforms.
